The Outdoor Brooklyn

Film Festival

August 5th to 8th @ Brooklyn Bridge Park

About Us

The 2019 Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival will run everyday from August 5th to August 8th from 6pm to midnight. We have selected a range of movies that guarantees there will be something to suit everyone's taste and appropriate to every age. The event will be held at the Brooklyn Bridge Park, along the East River. Space is limited to 5,000 people so pre registration is mandatory. Check out our movie selection, register, and prepare yourself to have a great time.

Day 1

5th August 2019
John Wick 3

Dr. Ryan Stone (Sandra Bullock) is a medical engineer on her first shuttle mission. Her commander is veteran astronaut Matt Kowalsky (George Clooney), helming his last flight before retirement. Then, during a routine space walk by the pair, disaster strikes: The shuttle is destroyed, leaving Ryan and Matt stranded in deep space with no link to Earth and no hope of rescue. As fear turns to panic, they realize that the only way home may be to venture further into space.
